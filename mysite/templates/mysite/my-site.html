Our Mission

At Code with Christ, we are striving to deliver an experience that allows students to learn web development skills, so that they can obtain a job that allows them to thrive. Exciting careers await those who are willing to work hard to learn to code.

We strive to be your professors who can help you learn any language you prefer to accomplish the tasks you are seeking to invest your time in. We will grow along with you as we seek to advance the Kingdom of God.
